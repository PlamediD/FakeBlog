Plamedi K. Diakubama

Quick description

Plamedi Diakubama is an international student from the Democratic Republic of the Congo. She was born and raised in the Kinshasa She permanently moved in the U.S in 2018 and started attending Bellevue College. She first started taking English classes and six months later started her associates degree. She graduated from B.C in 2021 with an Associates of Arts and Sciences with High distinction. She is a member of the Phi Theta Kappa Honnor Society

Plamedi was admitted for Fall 2021 at Seattle University in the Computer Science program. She was awarded the highest merit based scholarship, McGoldrick scholarship, Bannan scholarship and Alfie Scholarship
